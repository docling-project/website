date: 2021-11-01
venue: American Medical Informatics Association (AMIA) Annual Symposium 2021
summary: "Analyzes racial representation in dermatology academic materials. Studies have shown that images of skin of color are underrepresented in medical textbooks, academic curricula, and educational resources. This work quantitatively examines the prevalence of darker skin tones in dermatology educational materials and curricula. Findings indicate that representation of diverse skin tones remains critically low, with fewer textbooks having increased representation of dermatological diseases in darker skin tones over time. The research highlights the need for improved diversity and inclusion in medical education."
