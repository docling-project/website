title: BusiNet--a Light and Fast Text Detection Network for Business Documents
authors: O Naparstek, O Azulai, D Rotman, Y Burshtein, P Staar, U Barzelay
date: 2022-07-04
venue: arXiv
summary: "BusiNet is a detection network aimed at OCR of business documents. Business documents often include sensitive information and cannot be uploaded to cloud services for OCR. BusiNet was designed to be fast and light to run locally, preventing privacy issues. BusiNet is built to handle scanned document corruption and noise using a specialized synthetic dataset. The model is made robust to unseen noise by employing adversarial training strategies. The paper demonstrates the usefulness and broad applicability of the model on publicly available datasets."
