title: MolGrapher: Graph-based Visual Recognition of Chemical Structures
authors: L Morin, M Danelljan, MI Agea, A Nassar, V Weber, I Meijer, P Staar, F Yu
date: 2023-08-23
venue: ICCV
summary: "The automatic analysis of chemical literature has immense potential to accelerate the discovery of new materials and drugs. MolGrapher is introduced to recognize chemical structures visually using a deep keypoint detector to detect atoms, treating all candidate atoms and bonds as nodes in a graph, and classifying them with a Graph Neural Network. A synthetic data generation pipeline is proposed to address the lack of real training data, and a large-scale benchmark of annotated real molecule images (USPTO-30K) is introduced. Extensive experiments show significant improvements over classical and learning-based methods."
